3D SECURE

The "3D Secure" is a free-of-charge service facilitated by Visa and MasterCard to increase transactions security level when shopping online with your card.


As you will be automatically enrolled in the 3D secure service upon card activation, (you don't require any additional registration) you will receive a 6 digit One-Time Password via your preferred channel (either by SMS or email) for every online purchase you make with your card. All you have to do is enter the One-Time Password received to complete your transaction.

  • With the upgraded 3D Secure service you will receive a new one-time password, each time you make an online purchase with your card.
  • No need to worry about remembering your password anymore
  • The SMS or email upon which the One-Time Password will be sent to you, are the SMS and/or email registered at our end.

To ensure a hassle-free experience, please make sure that we have your updated contact details in our records:

  • Updated mobile number
  • Updated email address

If one of the above is missing, Kindly head to your branch to provide us with the needed information mentioned to make sure you receive a new One-Time Password each time you purchase online with your card.OR contact us on our website.
